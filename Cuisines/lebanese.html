Shahiat Tayiba! Welcome to Lebanese Cuisine

Lebanese cuisine is a vibrant fusion of Mediterranean flavors with
influences from the Middle East. Known for its fresh ingredients,
herbs, and spices, it offers a diverse array of dishes such
as falafel, hummus, tabbouleh, and kebabs. Olive oil, garlic, and lemon
are staples, while ingredients like pomegranate molasses and sumac
add depth to its rich culinary heritage.
