Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Pineapple Fried Rice:

    • 3 cups cooked jasmine rice, preferably chilled
    • 1 cup pineapple chunks, fresh or canned
    • 1 cup diced chicken or tofu
    • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
    • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 eggs, beaten
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • Chopped cilantro and green onions for garnish
    • 1 lime, cut into wedges for serving
    • Crushed peanuts for extra crunch (optional)

How to Make Thai Pineapple Fried Rice



To prepare Thai Pineapple Fried Rice:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.

  3. Add diced chicken or tofu, cook until browned and cooked through.

  4. Push the chicken or tofu to one side of the wok, pour beaten eggs on the other side, and scramble until cooked.

  5. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp.

  6. Add chilled cooked rice, soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar. Stir well to combine.

  7. Add pineapple chunks and continue to stir-fry until everything is heated through.

  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary.

  9. Serve hot, garnished with chopped cilantro and green onions.

  10. Squeeze lime wedges over the fried rice before eating for a burst of citrusy flavor.

  11. Sprinkle crushed peanuts on top for extra crunch if desired.
