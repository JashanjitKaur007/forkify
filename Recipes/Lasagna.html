Lasagna Recipe

An antipasto dish, Lasagna has grilled bread topped with veggies,
rubbed garlic and tomato mix. A country bread sliced and topped with
different toppings - the evergreen tomato-basil and an inventive
mushroom-garlic. The classic Italian starter! The key to making the
best Lasagna? It's all in the tomatoes. Because tomatoes are the focus
of this simple recipe, it’s worth it to use ripe, fresh ones. Juicier tomatoes work,
but drier varieties like Roma are really what you’re looking for.
The ultimate goal is to keep your toast like toast should be dry.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Lasagna:

      • 2 cans (800g) crushed tomatoes
      • 4 cloves garlic, minced
      • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
      • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
      • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
      • Salt and black pepper to taste
      • 1 pound (450g) pasta (e.g., spaghetti or linguine)
      • Salt for boiling water
      • Fresh basil leaves, torn
      • Grated Parmesan cheese
      • Extra-virgin olive oil for drizzling
      • Additional herbs for garnish (optional)
      • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
      • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
      • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)
      • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)

How to Make Lasagna



To prepare Lasagna:

  1. Cook 400g (14 oz) of spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.

  2. In a skillet, cook 200g (7 oz) of diced pancetta or guanciale until crispy.

  3. In a bowl, whisk together 3 large eggs, 1 cup (about 100g) of grated.

  4. Add minced garlic to the skillet and sauté until fragrant. Remove from heat.

  5. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the egg and cheese guanciale.

  6. Season with freshly ground black pepper and salt to taste.

  7. If desired, heat 1/4 cup (60ml) of extra-virgin olive oil

  8. Optionally, add 1 teaspoon of red pepper flakes for a bit of heat.

  9. Stir in 1 cup (240ml) of heavy cream for a richer sauce.

  10. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley, and serve.

  11. If desired, squeeze the juice.
