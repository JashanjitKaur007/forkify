Chili Lime Mushroom Tacos Recipe

Chili Lime Mushroom Tacos are a zesty and flavorful dish that
brings together the boldness of mushrooms with the tangy
kick of lime and chili. These tacos are a delightful choice for a
satisfying and spicy meal.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Chili Lime Mushroom Tacos:

    • 2 cups sliced mushrooms (e.g., cremini or button)
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 2 tablespoons chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1 tablespoon lime juice
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Tortillas for serving
    • Optional toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes,
    • avocado, cilantro, lime wedges

How to Make Chili Lime Mushroom Tacos



To prepare Chili Lime Mushroom Tacos:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.

  2. Add sliced mushrooms and saut√© until they release their moisture and become golden brown.

  3. In a small bowl, mix chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, and onion powder.

  4. Sprinkle the spice mixture over the mushrooms and toss to coat evenly.

  5. Drizzle lime juice over the mushrooms and continue to cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Warm tortillas and fill them with the chili lime mushrooms.

  8. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced avocado, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.

  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your Chili Lime Mushroom Tacos!
