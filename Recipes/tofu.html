Vegetarian/Vegan Tofu Thai Recipe

Vegetarian/Vegan Tofu Thai is a delightful dish featuring
stir-fried rice noodles with a medley of colorful vegetables
and a flavorful, tangy sauce. It's a perfect balance of sweet,
sour, and savory flavors without the use of animal products.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Vegetarian/Vegan Tofu Thai:

    • 200g (7 oz) rice noodles
    • 1 cup tofu, cubed
    • 1 cup broccoli florets
    • 1 cup carrots, julienned
    • 1 cup bean sprouts
    • 1/2 cup peanuts, crushed
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
    • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or agave nectar
    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 teaspoon chili flakes (adjust to taste)
    • Fresh cilantro for garnish
    • Lime wedges for serving

How to Make Vegetarian/Vegan Tofu Thai



To prepare Vegetarian/Vegan Tofu Thai:

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a wok or skillet, saut√© tofu until golden brown. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.

  3. Add broccoli, carrots, and bean sprouts to the wok. Stir-fry until vegetables are tender-crisp.

  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, tamarind paste, maple syrup, and chili flakes to create the sauce.

  5. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and tofu. Add cooked rice noodles and toss everything together.

  6. Top with crushed peanuts and garnish with fresh cilantro.

  7. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side.
