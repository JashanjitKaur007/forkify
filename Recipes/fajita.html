Veggie Fajitas Recipe

Veggie Fajitas are a delightful and colorful dish that brings together
a variety of fresh vegetables sautéed to perfection. This recipe is not
only delicious but also a great option for a healthy and vibrant meal.
Customize your fajitas with your favorite toppings and enjoy a burst of flavors!

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Veggie Fajitas:

    • 2 bell peppers (assorted colors), sliced
    • 1 large red onion, sliced
    • 1 zucchini, sliced
    • 1 yellow squash, sliced
    • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon fajita seasoning
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 8 small flour tortillas
    • Guacamole, salsa, sour cream for serving
    • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges for garnish

How to Make Veggie Fajitas



To prepare Veggie Fajitas:

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.

  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Add sliced bell peppers, red onion, zucchini, yellow squash, and cherry tomatoes to the skillet.

  4. Sprinkle fajita seasoning, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Warm the flour tortillas in a separate pan or microwave according to package instructions.

  6. Place a generous portion of the sautéed vegetables onto each tortilla.

  7. Top with guacamole, salsa, and sour cream as desired.

  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.

  9. Roll up the tortillas and enjoy your delicious Veggie Fajitas!

  10. Feel free to add your favorite cheese or avocado slices for extra creaminess.
  11. Experiment with different fajita seasoning blends for unique flavor profiles.
  12. Serve with a side of rice or black beans for a complete and satisfying meal.
