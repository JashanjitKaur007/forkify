Black Bean Tortilla Pie Recipe

Black Bean Tortilla Pie is a delicious and hearty dish that layers
flavors and textures in every bite. It’s one of my toddler’s favorite
meals. She always smiles when she sees it on the table.
Wendy Kelly, Petersburg, New York It combines the richness
of black beans, the freshness of vegetables, and the comfort of tortillas.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Black Bean Tortilla Pie:

    • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 cup corn kernels
    • 1 cup diced bell peppers (mixed colors)
    • 1 cup diced onions
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 6 large flour tortillas
    • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
    • 1 cup salsa
    • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • Fresh cilantro and sliced green onions for garnish

How to Make Black Bean Tortilla Pie



To prepare Black Bean Tortilla Pie:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, sauté onions and garlic until softened.

  3. Add bell peppers, corn, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
    Cook until vegetables are tender.

  4. Stir in black beans and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  5. Place one tortilla in the bottom of a greased pie dish.

  6. Layer with a portion of the bean and vegetable mixture, then sprinkle with cheese.

  7. Repeat layers, finishing with a tortilla on top.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing.

  10. Serve slices topped with salsa, sour cream, and garnish with fresh cilantro and sliced green onions.
