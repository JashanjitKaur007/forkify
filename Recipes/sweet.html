Sweet Potatoes with Cilantro Black Beans Recipe

Sweet Potatoes with Cilantro Black Beans is a delightful and
nutritious dish that combines the natural sweetness of sweet
potatoes with the savory goodness of cilantro-infused black beans.
As a vegan, I'm always looking for impressive dishes to share.
Sweet potatoes loaded with beans and a touch of peanut butter
are one of my mom’s favorites. —Kayla Capper, Ojai, California

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Sweet Potatoes with Cilantro Black Beans:

    • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
    • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 1 cup corn kernels
    • 1 cup diced bell peppers (mixed colors)
    • 1 cup diced onions
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 3 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
    • 6 large flour tortillas
    • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
    • 1 cup salsa
    • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • Fresh cilantro and sliced green onions for garnish
    • Avocado slices for serving
    • Lime wedges for serving

How to Make Sweet Potatoes with Cilantro Black Beans



To prepare Sweet Potatoes with Cilantro Black Beans:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. In a large skillet, sauté onions and garlic until softened.

  3. Add bell peppers, corn, ground cumin, chili powder, paprika.

  4. Add salt, and pepper. Cook until vegetables are tender.

  5. Stir in black beans and chopped cilantro, cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  6. Place diced sweet potatoes on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil.

  7. roast in the preheated oven until tender.

  8. In a separate pan, warm the tortillas.

  9. Assemble the dish by placing a tortilla on a plate

  10. adding a portion of roasted sweet potatoes, black bean mixture, and cheese.

  11. Repeat the layers.

  12. Serve topped with salsa, sour cream, avocado slices, fresh cilantro, and sliced green onions.

  13. Provide lime wedges on the side for a burst of citrus flavor.
