Hummus

Hummus is a delicious and nutritious dip or spread made from mashed
chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and salt. It is
a popular Middle Eastern dish, enjoyed for its creamy
texture and rich flavor.


To make hummus, simply blend all the ingredients together
until smooth and creamy. Adjust the seasoning to taste, adding
more lemon juice or salt if desired.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Hummus:

  • 1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini (sesame seed paste)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2-3 tablespoons water
  • Paprika and olive oil for garnish
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh mint
  • 1 tbsp finely diced red onion
  • 1 tbsp chopped Kalamata olives

How to Make Hummus



To prepare Hummus:

  1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon juice,
    olive oil, and salt.

  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, adding water as needed to achieve
    desired consistency.

  3. Taste and adjust seasoning, adding more lemon juice or salt if
    desired.

  4. Transfer hummus to a serving bowl and drizzle with olive oil.
    Sprinkle with paprika for garnish.

  5. Serve hummus with pita bread, crackers, or fresh vegetables.
