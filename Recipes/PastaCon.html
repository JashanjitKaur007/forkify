Pasta Con Pomodoro E Basilico Recipe

An antipasto dish, Pasta Con Pomodoro E Basilico has grilled bread
topped with veggies, rubbed garlic and tomato mix. A country bread
sliced and topped with different toppings - the evergreen tomato-basil
and an inventive mushroom-garlic. The classic Italian starter! The key
to making the best Pasta Con Pomodoro E Basilico? It's all in the
tomatoes. Because tomatoes are the focus of this simple recipe,
it’s worth it to use ripe, fresh ones. Juicier tomatoes work, but drier
varieties like Roma are really what you’re looking for.
The ultimate goal is to keep your toast like toast should be dry.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Pasta E Basilico:

    • 1 loaf of country bread
    • Cloves of garlic
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • Basil leaves
    • 5 Tbsp of olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • 1 pound (450g) pasta
    • Salt for boiling water
    • Fresh basil leaves, torn
    • Grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 garlic clove, grated
    • 4 portobello mushrooms, sliced
    • 1 red chilli, deseeded and chopped
    • 1 Tbsp butter

How to Make Pasta Con Pomodoro E Basilico



To prepare Pasta Con Pomodoro E Basilico:

  1. Boil 1 pound (450g) pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  2. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in 5 tablespoons of olive oil.

  3. Add 2 cans (800g) crushed tomatoes to the pan.

  4. Season with 1 teaspoon dried oregano, 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes.

  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the tomato sauce until well coated.

  6. Serve the pasta in a dish, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and drizzle.

  7. Optionally, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top for added flavor.

  8. Slice the tomatoes and Buffalo Mozzarella.

  9. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate, alternating with Cherry Tomatoes

  10. Sprinkle Tulsi and Red Onion on top.

  11. Optionally, garnish with additional Tulsi leaves.
