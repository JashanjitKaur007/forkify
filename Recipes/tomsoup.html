Tom Yum Soup With Coconut Milk (Tom Khaa) Recipe

Tom Yum Soup With Coconut Milk, or Tom Khaa, is a classic
Thai soup known for its aromatic blend of herbs and spices.
This soup features a rich coconut milk base with the tanginess
of lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal. It's a perfect balance
of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds. The key to an authentic
Tom Khaa is using fresh and quality ingredients.

Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Tom Yum Soup:

    • 4 cups vegetable broth
    • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
    • 200g (7 oz) shrimp, peeled and deveined
    • 200g (7 oz) chicken, thinly sliced
    • 1 stalk lemongrass, bruised and chopped
    • 3 kaffir lime leaves
    • 1 thumb-sized piece of galangal, sliced
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 red chili, sliced
    • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon lime juice
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
    • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish
    • Sliced red chili for garnish

How to Make Tom Yum Soup With Coconut Milk (Tom Khaa)



To prepare Tom Yum Soup With Coconut Milk:

  1. In a pot, bring the vegetable broth to a simmer.

  2. Add coconut milk, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal. Simmer for 10 minutes.

  3. Add minced garlic, sliced red chili, shrimp, and chicken. Cook until the protein is cooked through.

  4. Season with fish sauce, soy sauce, lime juice, and brown sugar. Adjust the seasoning to taste.

  5. Remove lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal slices before serving.

  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and sliced red chili.

  7. Serve hot and enjoy your Tom Yum Soup With Coconut Milk!
