Ingredients:


    Ingredients for Colorful Corn Salsa:

    • 2 cups corn kernels (fresh or frozen, thawed)
    • 1 cup diced tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup diced red onion
    • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers (mixed colors)
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro
    • 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional for spice)
    • Juice of 2 limes
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Tortilla chips for serving

How to Make Colorful Corn Salsa



To prepare Colorful Corn Salsa:

  1. In a large bowl, combine corn kernels, diced tomatoes, red onion, bell peppers, and cilantro.

  2. Add minced jalapeño for a spicy kick if desired.

  3. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

  4. Pour the dressing over the salsa and toss until well combined.

  5. Refrigerate the salsa for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

  6. Serve chilled, and garnish with additional cilantro if desired.

  7. Enjoy with tortilla chips or as a topping for grilled dishes and tacos.

  8. You can customize the salsa by adding diced avocados for creaminess.

  9. Experiment with different bell pepper colors to enhance visual appeal.

  10. For extra freshness, squeeze additional lime juice before serving.
